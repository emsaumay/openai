Shortcuts

  • Shortcut for seeking 10 seconds forward: Shift + →
  • Shortcut for seeking 10 seconds backward: Shift + ←
  • Shortcut for seeking 1 minute forward: Ctrl + →
  • Shortcut for seeking 1 minute backward: Ctrl + ←
  • Shortcut for changing playback speed: Ctrl + ↑ or Ctrl + ↓
